TC-Ratings

  • TC-Rating are the proprietary way the challenge rates each stat category
  • TC-Rating are calculated with linear interpolation comparing the current player's stat to the best in the league
  • The summary is adding all the the TC-ratings to a single number to compare
  • Who's better? The higher the better.
